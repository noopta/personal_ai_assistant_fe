We’re a family from Toronto with a big-hearted German Shepherd named Balto. Like most dogs, Balto lives for treats—but not just any treat. His absolute favourite? Freeze-dried chicken hearts.

We started noticing it was getting tough to find freeze-dried chicken hearts in stores around Toronto. And when we did find them, they were almost always made in the U.S.—not here in Canada. That didn’t sit right with us. We wanted something local, something we could trust, and something Balto would go crazy for.

So, we decided to make it ourselves.

At Balto Bites, we believe that every dog deserves the best. That's why we are dedicated to creating high-quality, natural dog treats that taste great and support your dog's health and well-being. Our flagship product, Balto Bites Chicken Hearts, is made from 100% pure chicken hearts, providing essential nutrients without any added flavours, colours, or preservatives. We are proud to manufacture our treats in Canada, ensuring that each bite meets our rigorous standards for quality and safety. Our eco-friendly packaging reflects our commitment to sustainability, helping to reduce our environmental footprint while delivering delicious and nutritious treats to your furry friends.

We understand the joy and companionship that dogs bring into our lives, and we strive to give back by offering treats that contribute to their happiness and health. Whether using our treats for training, as a reward, or as a meal topper, you can trust that Balto Bites will provide the wholesome goodness your dog deserves. Join us in our mission to make every dog a happy dog with Balto Bites. After all, a happy dog means a happy life.
